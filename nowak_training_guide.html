Managing Suppliers

Adding and editing your vendor partners

üè¢ What Are Suppliers?

Suppliers are the companies you buy products from. Before you can create purchase orders, you need suppliers in the system. Each supplier has contact info, payment terms, and links to their items.

Navigate to the Suppliers Tab

Click the "Suppliers" tab at the top of the screen.

You'll see a list of all existing suppliers in card format

Click "+ Add New Supplier"

Find the green button at the top of the supplier list and click it.

A form modal will appear with fields to fill out

Fill Out the Required Fields

Enter: Supplier Name (required), Contact Name, Phone, Email, Address, City, State, Zip. The Supplier ID is auto-generated.

Fields with red asterisks are required

Click "Save Supplier"

Review your entries and click the save button.

A success toast appears and the new supplier shows in the list
‚ö†Ô∏è

Important: Archiving vs Deleting

You cannot delete suppliers who have orders. Instead, archive them to hide them from active lists while preserving historical data.

Item Management

Your complete product catalog

üì¶ Understanding Items

Items are the products you buy and sell. Each item is linked to a supplier and has pricing, inventory levels, and reorder points. The system tracks stock automatically when you create orders.

Navigate to the Items Tab

Click "Items" in the top navigation.

You'll see item cards with photos, prices, and stock levels

Use the Filters

Try the Supplier dropdown to filter by vendor. Try the Stock Status filter to see "Low Stock" or "Out of Stock" items.

The list updates instantly as you filter

Understanding Stock Indicators

Green = In Stock, Yellow = Low Stock (below reorder point), Red = Out of Stock. Blue badge = quantity on order.

Visual cues make it easy to spot items needing attention
üí°

Pro Tip: Reorder Points

Set reorder points based on how fast items sell. The system will flag items when stock falls below this level!

Purchase Orders

Ordering inventory from suppliers

üõí The Purchasing Workflow

Purchase Orders (POs) are how you restock inventory. When you receive goods, the system automatically updates stock levels.

Go to the Purchases Tab

Click "Purchases" in the navigation.

You'll see existing POs with status badges

Click "+ Create Purchase Order"

Start a new purchase order.

Form opens asking for supplier first

Select a Supplier

Use the supplier picker modal. Only items from that supplier will be available.

Supplier info appears on the order

Add Line Items

Click the item picker to add products. Enter quantities.

Line totals calculate automatically

Save, Receive, and Pay

Save creates the PO. "Receive" updates inventory. "Record Payment" tracks payment status.

Complete workflow from order to payment!
